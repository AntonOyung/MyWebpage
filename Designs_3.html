Datafest: Infographic

On April 2017 a group of my friends attended the Berkeley Institute of Data Science's annual Datafest. The 3-day event hosted by graduate students was an opportunity to take the dataset that was given to us and see what we could come up with. After analyzing the data, we created this infographic with our findings which won us the Best Visualization award.

Our code is accessible on github.
