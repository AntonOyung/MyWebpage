This is my family and me at my most recent Dragonboat race (I'm the one with warpaint on my face).

Dragonboat is an ancient Chinese sport that can most easily be described as competetive canoeing. Every Saturday and Sunday 40+ of us meet up at the Jack London Aquatic Center in Oakland to paddle around in large 20-person dragonboats. As strange as it sounds, I could not be more blessed to find such a strong, supportive and loving community at Cal during my first semester. What makes me love this goofy, quirky, and perfectly imperfect group of paddlers is their love for each other and the sport. Nobody joins dragonboat to pad their resume or land an internship, but rather everyone shows up solely for the community and to practice the sport they love.
