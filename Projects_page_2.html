Binwise Inc.

My 2016 summer I worked as a statistical programmer intern at a small SF-based company called Binwise. Binwise, a wine inventory tracking B2B company, deals with the sales data from top restaurants all across the nation. During my time at Binwise I was purposed with the task of analyzing the sales data. I taught myself to use tools such as SQL Server Management Studio and Rstudio and utilized Python in order to produce revenue generating business intelligence reports and model sales data. Working at Binwise was an excellent opportunity for me to get work experience in a real dev environment and the intimate corporate atmosphere allowed me to interact closely with everyone on the dev team.
